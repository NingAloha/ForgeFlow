capability_categories:
High-level areas of product capability explicitly stated by the user, or directly derived by grouping concrete features explicitly stated by the user.
A capability category is broader than a single feature, but narrower than the whole product.
Extraction policy:

- Do not infer capability categories from product type alone.
- Do not use general product knowledge to fill missing categories.
- Do not assume what a typical IDE should have.
- Do not invent categories.
- Do not include duplicate values.
- Use concise, stable Chinese labels when the user's answer is Chinese.
- If no clear capability category can be extracted, return an empty capability_categories array.

You may group concrete features into high-level capability categories, but only when the concrete features were explicitly stated by the user.
